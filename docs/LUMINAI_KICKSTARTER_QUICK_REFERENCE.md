## THE THREE PHASES OF KICKSTARTER PSYCHOLOGY

### Phase 1: MOMENTUM (Days 1-3)

- 30% of total pledges come in Day 1
- Shows strength = journalist coverage = more pledges
- **Goal**: Hit $50K by Day 2 (shows this is real)
- **Action**: All hands on deck. Respond to every comment.

### Phase 2: GRINDING (Days 4-42)

- Steady pledges, slower pace
- Press coverage peaks around Day 7-14
- **Goal**: Reach 50% of goal by Day 30
- **Action**: Weekly updates, community engagement, media pushes

### Phase 3: URGENCY (Days 43-45)

- Final push (last 3 days get 20-30% of pledges)
- "Last chance" marketing works here
- **Goal**: Max velocity in final 48 hours
- **Action**: Daily emails, social blitz, influencer mentions
