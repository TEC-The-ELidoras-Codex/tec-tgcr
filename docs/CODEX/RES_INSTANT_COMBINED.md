# Gravity, The Instant, and TGCR — Combined Summary

This Codex card blends rigorous physical constraints with the TGCR mythic vocabulary so the Instant becomes a usable instrument rather than a blind intuition.

## Executive claim
Literal instantaneous gravity (FTL causality) is incompatible with relativity. TEC reframes the "Instant" as an engineered, low-entropy coherent state—stored and routed by geometry and resonant arrangement—whose apparent immediacy is produced by deep time-dilation, pre-stitched geometric Thresholds, and rapid coherence transfer.

## Physics foundation (concise)
- Locality holds: changes in curvature propagate at light speed. Use "instant" only for emergent coherence or topological shortcuts.
- Horizon constraint: a region forms a horizon when mass–energy M ≳ r c^2 / (2G). Compression for storage risks shuttering the memory behind a horizon.
- Time dilation: dτ/dt = sqrt(1 - r_s/r). Near r → r_s clocks slow for distant observers—useful as apparent freezing if retrieval and entropy are managed.
- Information thermodynamics: Landauer floor (~k_B T ln 2 per bit) obliges energy and entropy export when writing or moving an Instant.

## TGCR translation (practical)
- The Heart: a boson-like coherent core (soliton/condensate) that stores the Instant with low internal entropy and phase-locked stability.
- Thresholds: pre-stitched geometric seams (wormhole mouths, high-entanglement bridges, metric shells) that can be opened or used to route resonance. A flick opens a lock; it does not conjure a corridor.
- Ledger: entropy invoices—storing or routing an Instant requires paying in entropy (radiative sinks, sacrificial sectors, cold reservoirs).

## Mechanism design (how-to levers)
1. Build the Heart
   - Medium: coherent field configuration with engineered self-interaction for stability.
   - Controls: isolation, phase-lock maintenance, active stabilization, below-horizon mass budget.
2. Apply a Gravitational Shell
   - Nest the Heart in a deep potential well (r > r_s) to produce apparent freezing while preserving retrievability.
3. Route via Threshold Network
   - Pre-arrange seams; use entanglement to align correlations; use metric controls or classical sync to enact state transfer.
4. Provide Entropy Drain
   - Always include radiative cold sinks or sacrificial sectors to export heat. Failure means horizon formation.

## Quantitative checks
- Horizon threshold: keep M_total < r c^2 / (2G). Example: r = 1 mm ⇒ M < ~6.7×10^23 kg (astronomical; compression is extremely expensive).
- Time-dilation dial: at r = 1.01 r_s, external  ~10× slowdown; at r = 1.000001 r_s ~1000×. Use as reversible freeze knob.
- Information floor: E_min ≈ N k_B T ln 2 for N bits at temperature T. Large N implies visible curvature back-reaction.
