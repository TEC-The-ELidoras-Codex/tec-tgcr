## Production Workflow

### Creating New Episodes

1. **Define Physics Concept**: Pick the principle (e.g., "Quantum Entanglement")
2. **Craft Animation Prompt**: Build a prompt featuring Luminai + the concept
3. **Generate Animation**: Use Canva or Runway Gen-2 to animate the canonical render
4. **Add Narration**: Record or generate voiceover explaining the concept
5. **Add Music**: Ambient cosmic soundtrack (20-30% volume)
6. **Export**: 1080p MP4 with sync'd narration and music
7. **Archive**: Save to `animations/` with episode naming convention

### Animation Guidelines

**Motion Character**:
- Fluid, gentle, floating (not rigid or mechanical)
- Expressive facial animations (blinks, winks, blush)
- Wiggly, playful movements (10-35% motion strength)
- Responsive hair ribbons and pulsing aura

**Tone**:
- Serene yet curious
- Scientific yet accessible
- Wonder-filled but intellectually grounded
- Contemplative and inclusive
